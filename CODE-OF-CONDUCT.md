# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and community members.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional in all interactions
- Welcome diverse perspectives and experiences
- Provide constructive feedback
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy toward other community members

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or bullying of any kind
- Offensive comments related to identity or expression
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling or deliberate disruption
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Violations of this code of conduct may result in:
- Warning or temporary suspension
- Removal from the community
- Blocking from the repository

## Reporting

Please report incidents to the maintainers via email or by opening a private issue. All reports will be treated confidentially.
